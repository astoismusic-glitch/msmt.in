About artist

AstroManish is India based indie artist.

His music is all about his own self. He moves through multiple genres like EDM, R&B, pop, hoodtrap, trap and folk, while keeping the writing personal and honest.

He produces, writes, performs, mixes and masters his own music. He also produces for other artists in his local scene.
